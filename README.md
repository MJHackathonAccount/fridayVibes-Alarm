1. **Use multiple sound types** - Different frequencies wake different people
2. **Test your setup** - Make sure sounds are loud enough but not speaker-damaging
3. **Set meaningful goals** - Use the daily goals to create positive morning motivation  
4. **Go full screen** - The immersive experience is more effective

### Sound Selection Strategy:
- **High-frequency sounds** (smoke detector, baby crying) for deep sleepers
- **Low-frequency sounds** (air horn, train horn) for bass sensitivity
- **Urgent sounds** (sirens, alarms) trigger natural wake-up response
- **Annoying sounds** (construction, drilling) impossible to ignore
