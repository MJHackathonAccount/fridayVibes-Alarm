#### **Frequency Strategy:**
- **High-pitched sounds** (2000-8000 Hz) - Best for deep sleepers
- **Low-pitched sounds** (100-500 Hz) - Penetrating bass tones
- **Mix both** for maximum effectiveness!

#### **File Optimization:**
- **Keep files under 5MB** for fast loading
- **30 seconds to 2 minutes** is perfect length (will loop)
- **MP3 format** works best for compatibility
- **Test volume levels** - loud but not speaker-damaging
